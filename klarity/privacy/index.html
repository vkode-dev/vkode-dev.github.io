Klarity respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Important: We use AI on our servers only when you use the Summarize feature. For this feature, the text you choose to submit is sent to our server temporarily for processing. Voice to text, transcription, and document scanning are processed on your device and are not sent to any server. We do not store any text or recordings on our servers. Our AI processing partner may store and process summary requests in accordance with their own policies.
  • Device information such as operating system and device type is collected by Firebase Crashlytics only to detect and report crashes. We do not collect personal identifiers or track your activity.
  • Document scanning and extracted text are processed locally on your device.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process text you submit for Summarize. This text is sent temporarily to our server for processing and not stored by us.
  • Voice recordings and scanned documents are processed locally on your device. They are never transmitted to any server unless you explicitly send text through an AI feature such as Summarize.
  • To improve app functionality using anonymized crash reports provided through Firebase Crashlytics.

Advertisements

  • Klarity displays banner and interstitial advertisements using Google AdMob.
  • Google AdMob may use device identifiers and cookies to show relevant ads. No transcriptions, recordings, scanned documents, or user generated content are shared with AdMob.
  • You may review Google's Privacy Policy at: https://policies.google.com/privacy .

Data Sharing

  • We do not track users or collect personal analytics data.
  • Crash reports are handled through Firebase Crashlytics and do not include personal user content.

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Crashlytics is used to detect and report crashes.
  • Google AdMob is used to display advertisements.
  • An AI service provider processes text when you use features such as Summarize. No audio or documents are transmitted unless converted to text and explicitly submitted by you.

Data Security

  • We implement reasonable industry standard security measures to protect your information.
  • We do not store your recordings, scanned documents, or transcribed text on our servers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

  • We may update this privacy policy from time to time.
  • Significant updates will be announced through the app.
