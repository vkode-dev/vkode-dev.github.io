Klarity respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Important: Your voice recordings and meeting notes remain completely on your device. Only the transcribed text from "Grammar" or "Summarize" features is sent to the server for further processing.
  • Device information (e.g., operating system, device type) is collected only for app functionality and to report crashes via Firebase Crashlytics. No personal data or activity is tracked.

How We Use Your Information

  • Process your transcribed text to provide grammar correction, summaries, and translations using GPT, a language model developed by OpenAI. Only the text from "Grammar" or "Summarize" features is sent to the server. We do not store any data on our servers.
  • Voice recordings are processed locally on your device and never transmitted to any server.
  • The recordings are deleted after processing.
  • Improve the app's functionality and performance using anonymized crash and error reports via Firebase Crashlytics.
